Introducing Droplit:
The Smart Showerhead

The more we save, the more we can GIVE

By reducing your shower times by 8 minutes, you’re saving over 18 gallons of water. We take your shower times and results each 6 months, and develop water filtration systems in developing countries with the profits.

See Our Cause

Help us save our most precious resource:

WATER

Beautifully designed,beautifully FUNCTIONAL

Our bluetooth and water powered showerhead notices how much water your using and allows you to set personal goals to reduce your water waste. Colored LEDs let you know how much time you have left and where you are in the shower cycle.
