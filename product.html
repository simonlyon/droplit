How It Works

Droplit is an environmentally friendly shower head designed to create awareness around water consumption.



Install

Unscrew your existing shower head, then screw your new Droplit shower head right on. It’s that simple.




Connect

Connect your Droplit to the mobile app by running the water through it and following the in app prompts.




Save

Set your Droplit shower time, and start saving water and money immediately.


Set Your Goals

Droplit allows you to easily set goals by adjusting the amount of time you spend in shower. Giving you the control.

Track Your Usage

Through an intuitive dashboard, Droplit allows you to track your water use in real time.

See the Results

By displaying information in easily understandable terms, Droplit is providing a greater understanding of your individual impact.

Download the App

Our app is available for download from the Google Play and Apple stores, free of charge.
