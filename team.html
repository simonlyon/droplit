About Droplit and Re:Studio

Droplit is a product designed by the students of Re:Studio, the product incubator at IXDMA. We are a one-year, intensive, professional masters program dedicated to re:designing, re:imagining, and re:working the digital world. Now accepting applications for the 2017-2018 academic year.
