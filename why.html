At Droplit, we believe that big waves are made with small actions.

The Droplit shower head promotes awareness around water use, helps reduce water waste, and provides access to fresh water around the world.

Giving Back

Droplit is committed to making a difference in local communities. For every 500 units Droplit sells, we fund a global water project for a community in need.
