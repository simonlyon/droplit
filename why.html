Our Mission

The future of conscious showering

At Droplit we believe that change starts in the home and that big waves are made with small actions.

The Droplit showerhead promotes awareness around your water usage, helps you reduce your water waste, and provides greater access to fresh water around the world.

Giving Back

Droplit is committed to making a difference in local communities. For every 500 units Droplit sells, we fund a global water project for a community in need.


Join us in our mission to help raise awareness about our water use and help solve water issues globally.

See Change in Action

We partnered with The Water Project to provide Gravity Well Pumps to communities in need. With the profits made from selling Droplit, we help foster free access to clean water around the world.
